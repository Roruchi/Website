# Platform Engineering Foundations

**Duration:** 2 days (16 hours)  
**Audience:** Platform engineers, DevOps engineers, engineering managers  
**Format:** In-person or virtual (max 20 participants)

## Overview

This hands-on workshop gives your team the foundations to build and operate an effective Internal Developer Platform. We combine theory with practical exercises, leaving participants with actionable plans for their own organizations.

## What You'll Learn

### Day 1: Strategy & Discovery
- What is platform engineering (and what it isn't)
- The CNCF platforms maturity model
- Running developer experience research: interviews, surveys, SPACE metrics
- Defining your IDP vision and roadmap
- **Workshop:** Map your current developer journey and identify key pain points

### Day 2: Building & Measuring
- Designing golden paths and paved roads
- Self-service infrastructure with Backstage and Crossplane
- GitOps for platform delivery
- Measuring adoption and developer satisfaction
- **Workshop:** Build a minimal golden path for a sample service

## Prerequisites

- Basic understanding of Kubernetes and CI/CD concepts
- Familiarity with cloud infrastructure (any major cloud)
- No prior platform engineering experience required

## What's Included

- Pre-workshop assessment to tailor content to your team
- All workshop materials and hands-on exercises
- Reference architecture templates
- 30-day follow-up Q&A session
- Participant workbooks

## Outcomes

After completing this workshop, your team will:
- Have a shared vocabulary and vision for platform engineering
- Understand how to prioritize platform investments
- Have a concrete roadmap for your first or next IDP iteration
- Know how to measure and communicate platform value
