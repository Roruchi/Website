# Developer Experience Masterclass

**Duration:** 1 day (8 hours)  
**Audience:** Platform engineers, team leads, developer advocates, engineering managers  
**Format:** In-person or virtual (max 24 participants)

## Overview

Developer experience (DevEx) is increasingly recognized as a key driver of engineering productivity and talent retention — but it's often treated as vague or unmeasurable. This masterclass gives you concrete frameworks and tools to measure, improve, and advocate for DevEx in your organization.

## Program

### Morning: Understanding & Measuring DevEx
- Defining developer experience: beyond "ergonomics"
- The SPACE framework for measuring developer productivity
- Running developer experience research:
  - User interviews and contextual inquiry
  - Developer satisfaction surveys
  - Journey mapping
- Key DevEx metrics: onboarding time, build times, deployment frequency, etc.
- **Workshop:** Run a mini DevEx audit using real data

### Afternoon: Improving & Advocating
- Identifying and prioritizing DevEx improvements
- The "paved road" principle: making the right thing easy
- Communicating DevEx ROI to leadership
- Building a DevEx culture: champions, working groups, feedback loops
- Case studies: DevEx transformations at real companies
- **Workshop:** Create a DevEx improvement roadmap for your team

## Prerequisites

- Experience working in or with software engineering teams
- No specific technical background required

## What's Included

- Workshop materials and templates
- DevEx survey template (ready to deploy)
- Developer journey map canvas
- DevEx metrics dashboard template (for Grafana/Datadog)

## Outcomes

Participants will leave with:
- A clear definition and measurement framework for DevEx
- Research skills to understand their developers' needs
- A prioritized improvement roadmap
- Tools and templates to track and communicate progress
