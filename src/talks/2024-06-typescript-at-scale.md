# TypeScript at Scale: Patterns for Large Codebases

**Event:** TSConf 2024  
**Date:** June 14, 2024  
**Location:** Seattle, WA, USA

## Abstract

TypeScript is fantastic for small projects. But as your codebase grows to hundreds of files and dozens of contributors, new challenges emerge: slow type-checking, overly complex types, inconsistent patterns, and increasing build times.

This talk explores practical patterns for keeping large TypeScript codebases maintainable and performant, drawing from real-world experience at companies of different scales.

## Topics Covered

- Project references for faster incremental builds
- Architectural patterns for keeping types clean
- When to use generics vs. overloads vs. conditional types
- Tooling and automation for enforcing consistency
- Common pitfalls and how to avoid them
