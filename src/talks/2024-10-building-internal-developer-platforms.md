# Building Internal Developer Platforms That Developers Actually Love

**Event:** PlatformCon 2024  
**Date:** October 22, 2024  
**Location:** London, UK

## Abstract

Internal Developer Platforms (IDPs) promise to reduce cognitive load and increase developer velocity — but too often they're built by platform teams for platform teams, not for the developers who use them daily.

In this talk, I share lessons learned from building and rolling out IDPs at scale, focusing on how to apply product thinking to platform development, gather meaningful developer feedback, and iterate quickly.

## Key Takeaways

- Why empathy is the most important skill in platform engineering
- How to run effective developer experience research
- The pitfalls of building features nobody asked for
- Measuring platform adoption and developer satisfaction
