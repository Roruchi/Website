# GitOps: The Good, The Bad, and The GitOps

**Event:** KubeCon North America 2023  
**Date:** November 9, 2023  
**Location:** Chicago, IL, USA

## Abstract

Everyone is talking about GitOps. But between the blog posts and vendor marketing, it can be hard to separate the signal from the noise. This talk gives an honest, experience-based assessment of GitOps in practice.

We cover the genuine benefits, the real pain points, common misconceptions, and practical advice for teams evaluating or adopting GitOps.

## Key Points

- What GitOps actually means (and what it doesn't)
- Real benefits: auditability, recovery, multi-cluster management
- Real challenges: secrets management, debugging drift, operational complexity
- ArgoCD vs Flux: an honest comparison
- Migration strategies for existing teams
